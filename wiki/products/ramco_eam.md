# Ramco EAM

**Product ID:** `ramco_eam`  
**Category:** ERP  
**Tagline:** Enterprise Asset Management for asset-intensive operations.

## Description

Ramco EAM (Enterprise Asset Management) helps asset-intensive organizations manage the full asset lifecycle: maintenance planning, work orders, spares inventory, and reliability — with mobility and predictive-maintenance insights. It is often deployed alongside Ramco ERP.

## Target Audience

Asset-intensive operators (utilities, infrastructure, equipment fleets, facilities).

## Pricing

Not published publicly. Licensed via a custom enterprise quote. Contact Ramco sales.

## Features

- Asset register and lifecycle tracking
- Preventive and predictive maintenance
- Work order and resource scheduling
- Spares and inventory management
- Maintenance mobility apps
- Reliability and cost analytics

## Integrations

- REST web services API
- Ramco ERP
- IoT / sensor data feeds
- Finance back-ends
