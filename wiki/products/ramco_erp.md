# Ramco ERP

**Product ID:** `ramco_erp`  
**Category:** ERP  
**Tagline:** Cloud-native, AI-powered ERP for mid-to-large enterprises.

## Description

Ramco ERP is an integrated, cloud-native enterprise resource planning suite covering finance, supply chain, manufacturing, procurement, sales, and project management. It uses a unified data model with embedded analytics and context-aware automation so business processes can span modules without custom integration.

## Target Audience

Mid-to-large enterprises in manufacturing, services, and distribution that want a single integrated ERP on the cloud.

## Pricing

Not published publicly. Ramco ERP is licensed via a custom enterprise quote based on modules, users, and deployment. Contact Ramco sales.

## Features

- Finance & accounting (GL, AP/AR, multi-currency, multi-GAAP)
- Supply chain and inventory management
- Manufacturing and production planning
- Procurement and vendor management
- Project management and costing
- Embedded analytics and role-based dashboards
- Context-aware in-app automation (Ramco's 'cognitive' workflows)

## Integrations

- REST/SOAP web services API
- Salesforce
- Microsoft 365 / Excel
- Bank payment gateways
- Tax e-invoicing networks
