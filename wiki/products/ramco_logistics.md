# Ramco Logistics

**Product ID:** `ramco_logistics`  
**Category:** Logistics  
**Tagline:** Logistics & Transportation Management for 3PL and freight.

## Description

Ramco Logistics is a transportation and logistics management suite for third-party logistics (3PL), freight forwarding, and courier/express businesses. It covers order and contract management, billing, fleet and route optimization, and digital proof-of-delivery on a single platform.

## Target Audience

3PL providers, freight forwarders, and courier/express/parcel companies.

## Pricing

Not published publicly. Licensed via a custom enterprise quote. Contact Ramco sales.

## Features

- Order and contract management
- Rating, billing, and settlement
- Fleet and route optimization
- Warehouse and inventory visibility
- Digital proof-of-delivery (mobile)
- Logistics analytics and dashboards

## Integrations

- REST web services API
- Telematics / GPS providers
- E-commerce marketplaces
- Finance/ERP back-ends
