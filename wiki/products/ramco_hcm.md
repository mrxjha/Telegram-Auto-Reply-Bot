# Ramco HCM & Global Payroll

**Product ID:** `ramco_hcm`  
**Category:** HCM  
**Tagline:** Unified HR and multi-country payroll across 100+ countries.

## Description

Ramco HCM is a unified Human Capital Management and Global Payroll platform covering core HR, time & attendance, talent management, and statutory-compliant payroll across 100+ countries from a single system. It is known for its multi-country payroll engine and chatbot-driven employee experience.

## Target Audience

Multinational employers and shared-service / payroll-outsourcing providers needing consolidated multi-country payroll and HR.

## Pricing

Not published publicly. Priced per employee/per country via a custom enterprise quote. Contact Ramco sales.

## Features

- Core HR and employee master data
- Global payroll across 100+ countries with local statutory compliance
- Time & attendance and leave management
- Talent management (recruitment, performance, learning)
- Employee self-service with conversational chatbot
- Payroll analytics and compliance reporting

## Integrations

- REST web services API
- Microsoft 365
- Time-clock / biometric devices
- Banking & payment files
- Third-party benefits providers
