# Ramco Aviation (M&E / MRO)

**Product ID:** `ramco_aviation`  
**Category:** Aviation  
**Tagline:** Aviation, Aerospace & Defense maintenance and MRO software.

## Description

Ramco Aviation is a comprehensive Maintenance & Engineering (M&E) and Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul (MRO) suite for airlines, MROs, heli-operators, and defense fleets. It covers airworthiness, line and base maintenance, component overhaul, supply chain, and compliance, with mobility and AI-assisted maintenance.

## Target Audience

Airlines, independent MRO providers, helicopter operators, and aerospace & defense fleet operators.

## Pricing

Not published publicly. Licensed via a custom enterprise quote based on fleet size and modules. Contact Ramco sales.

## Features

- Airworthiness and continuing-airworthiness management
- Line, base, and shop (component) maintenance
- MRO supply chain and inventory
- Compliance with aviation regulatory frameworks
- Maintenance mobility apps (offline-capable)
- AI-assisted maintenance and anomaly insights

## Integrations

- REST web services API
- OEM data feeds
- Flight operations systems
- Finance/ERP back-ends
