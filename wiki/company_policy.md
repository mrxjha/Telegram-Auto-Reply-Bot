# Ramco Systems — Company Policies

_Enterprise software for the cloud era — ERP, HCM & Payroll, Aviation, and Logistics._

Ramco Systems is a global enterprise software provider best known for its cloud-native ERP, Global Payroll & HCM, Aviation/Aerospace & Defense (M&E/MRO), and Logistics solutions. Ramco software is delivered on the Ramco platform with embedded AI/ML, and is used by enterprises across 150+ countries.

## At a Glance
- **Founded:** 1997
- **Headquarters:** Chennai, India
- **Billing currency:** Varies by region / contract

## Support
- **Contact:** Refer to your Ramco account manager or the public contact form at ramco.com/contact
- **Hours:** Enterprise support per the signed service agreement (regional coverage; 24x7 available on eligible plans)

## Pricing
- Ramco does not publish list pricing. All pricing, licensing tiers, and discounts are provided by Ramco sales as a custom enterprise quote.
- **Important:** there is no public list price. Do NOT quote or estimate a price; direct the customer to Ramco sales for a quote.

## Refund and Contract
- Refunds, cancellation, and renewal terms are governed by the customer's signed enterprise agreement (MSA/order form); there is no public, one-size-fits-all refund window.
- **Public refund window:** none — governed by the signed contract.
- **Free trial:** no standard public free trial; demos are arranged via Ramco sales.
